Our Lady of Mount Carmel

Date of Origin

Devotion began in the 12th century

Role

Title given to the Virgin Mary as patroness of the Carmelite Order

Notable

Our Lady of Mount Carmel is a deeply venerated Marian title in Malta, especially among the Carmelite communities. She is the patroness of the Carmelite Church in Valletta, known for its iconic dome and rich religious heritage. The devotion is celebrated annually on July 16, drawing large crowds and processions. The title symbolizes protection, contemplation, and spiritual guidance, and holds a special place in Maltese Catholic tradition.
