Ġilormu Cassar gilormu

Born: c. 1520

Role: Maltese architect and military engineer

Notable: Took over from Laparelli and designed most of Valletta’s major buildings, including St. John's Co-Cathedral, the Grandmaster’s Palace, and the Auberges. He is Malta’s most important Renaissance architect.
